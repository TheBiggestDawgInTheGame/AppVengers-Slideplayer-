About SidePlay

SlidePlay is a gamified learning platform that transforms study materials into interactive mini-games. By combining AI-driven quiz generation, document scanning, and immersive game mechanics, it creates a dynamic, engaging, and socially connected learning experience.

Our Mission

We aim to create clean, accessible, and modern web experiences by combining thoughtful design with reliable functionality.

Who We Are

Our team is dedicated to building user-centered pages that are easy to navigate and visually engaging. We focus on delivering useful features in every release.

Key Features

  • Stimulating and interactive games that incorporate studying and active learning in them
  • A tool that allows lecturers to monitor their students' progress.
  • Students can compete in various challenges and tournaments with their classmates and friends.
  • Students can look at their ranking on the leaderboard and track their own progress.
