# Scramble Game

A scrambled word and sentence game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Features

- 3 difficulty levels:
  - **Easy**: Unscramble a single scrambled word
  - **Medium**: Unscramble a shuffled sentence
  - **Hard**: Unscramble a scrambled paragraph with a 3-minute timer
- Dark theme with magenta and cyan accent colors
- Immediate feedback after submission
- Next button to move through scrambled items

## How to Play

1. Open `scramble_game.html` in your browser
2. Choose a difficulty level
3. Unscramble the displayed text and type your answer
4. Press **Submit** to check your guess
5. Press **Next** to try another scramble

## Notes

- The hardest level includes a **3-minute timer** for the paragraph challenge
- The harder the level, the more scrambled and longer the text becomes
- Use the **Easy** and **Medium** levels to warm up before the paragraph challenge

## Customization

Edit `script.js` to add or change the scrambled content in `gameData`.

- Add more words in `easy`
- Add more sentences in `medium`
- Add more paragraphs in `hard`

Enjoy the game! 🎉
