    In-Game Help

    🎮 Getting Started

    • Pick an Operator on the Character screen — each has unique abilities and difficulty.
    • On the Mission screen, choose a World to enter. Harder worlds award more points.
    • Worlds unlock in order — complete earlier ones to open the next.

    ⭐ Points & Progress

    • Each completed world earns 120 pts (180 in Co-op mode).
    • Bonus points are awarded for collectibles found inside a world.
    • Progress saves automatically — use Save / Resume for manual slots.

    🔥 Daily Challenge

    • A new challenge appears each day. Accept it to start tracking.
    • Complete the challenge conditions during your next world run to earn bonus points.
    • Completing challenges on consecutive days builds your Streak multiplier.

    🤝 Co-op Mode

    • Toggle Co-op from the top bar before launching a world.
    • Co-op runs award extra points and unlock the Team Player achievement.

    ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

    • Esc — Close any open modal
    • Tab — Navigate world cards on the Mission screen
    • Enter — Select focused world card
